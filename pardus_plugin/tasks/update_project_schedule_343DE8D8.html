    Main Description
    The project schedule should be updated in each of every technical meetings with the participation of the person or people responsible for coding, if necessary the date shifting or postponing conditions should be comitted to the calendar with the approval of the person or people claiming the project. T his processes are performed by the project leader. This calendar should be operated on Pardus Project Tracking System (tracker.pardus.org.tr) and it should be known that the entire development team will continue to the task sharing through this calendar. T he responsibility of the unspecified conditions in project tracking system belong to the person who have been on that part of the task. The person/people who demands the project also could get the project status information from the system instantaneously.
