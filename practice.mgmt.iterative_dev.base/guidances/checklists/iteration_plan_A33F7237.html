Checklist: Iteration Plan
This checklist provides questions to verify that iteration plans are created in a consistent manner.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Iteration Plan
Check Items
Have you elaborated on the plan with the team?

Iteration planning should not be done in isolation, and every member of the team should be involved.

Are iteration objectives clear?
  • Are the objectives based on stakeholder priorities?
  • Do they help the team target what should be targeted for the iteration?
Have key milestones for the iteration been identified?

This may include dates for iteration reviews, demonstrations (demos), and retrospective reviews.

Do the team members feel confident about their Work Item assignments?

Looking at results from previous iterations, does the amount of work selected for the current iteration seem feasible?

If the team feels that there is too much to do, discuss with stakeholders whether some work items can be scheduled for future iterations.
