Main Description

Example to breakdown rule into atomic level:



"The credit card used to guarantee a rental must belong to one of the authorized drivers; and this driver must sign the rental agreement. Other drivers must sign an additional drivers authorization form"

The above rule can be broken down to following rules:



R1: The credit card used to guarantee a rental must belong to one of the authorized drivers



R2: driver must sign the rental agreement



R3: Other drivers must sign an additional drivers authorization form
